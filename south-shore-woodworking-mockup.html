Brand Activations

We build brand activations that earn a second look.

Custom fabrication for the experiential moments brands stage — engineered for foot traffic, fast install, and brand precision. Built in our Center Moriches shop, delivered and installed across the NYC tri-state.

The build that pulls people into the experience.

South Shore Woodworking designs and fabricates the physical pieces that make activations work. Photo moments, dimensional logos, branded archways, interactive walls, oversized props — every piece engineered for the realities of a live activation: weight, transport, install windows, brand standards, weather.

We work directly with brand activation agencies, experiential marketing firms, in-house corporate marketing teams, and the artists and creative directors who hire us direct. Projects typically run $20,000 to $40,000+, with timelines from concept to install ranging from 3 to 8 weeks.

For Agencies

For Brands

How we work.

From brief to truck. Six steps. One point of contact — Alex.

01 / Brief

Send us anything.

Deck, sketch, paragraph, PO. We respond inside one business day with feasibility, ballpark, and the questions we need answered.

02 / Design

Build drawings & specs.

Material specs, hardware schedule, structural detail, finish schedule. We work from your renderings or our own.

03 / Approve

Sign-off & schedule.

Sign-off on drawings, finishes, timeline. Deposit triggers material order and shop slot.

04 / Build

Fabricate, finish, QC.

Daily photos on request. We don't ship anything we wouldn't put our name on. Full mock-up before the truck loads.

Pop-Ups & Retail Display

We build pop-ups that ship on a tight calendar.

Full retail environments and fixture programs built to brand standards, transport flat, and assemble fast — for direct-to-consumer pop-ups, brand activations, and short-run retail moments across the NYC tri-state.

Retail-grade. Activation-fast.

Pop-up retail is built different. The fixtures have to look permanent, but the build has to assemble in 48 hours and strike inside one. We design and fabricate complete pop-up programs — fixtures, cash-wraps, signage walls, product plinths, cladding, lighting integration — engineered for the install window and the brand standard.

We work with brand teams running their own pop-ups, experiential agencies producing brand-led retail, and DTC brands testing IRL. Most projects ship as modular kits with documented assembly so your install crew (or ours) can put it up clean.

Stages & DJ Booths

We build stages and DJ booths that hold up to the night.

Performance-ready stages and DJ booths designed for tour-grade durability, clean cable management, and serious visual presence — for venues, touring artists, and one-off branded productions.

Built to perform. Built to travel.

DJ booths and stage builds are a specific kind of fabrication problem. They have to look perfect for the photo, survive the night for the crowd, break down for the truck, and hold up across the tour. We build for all four — modular construction, transport-safe finishes, integrated cable pass-throughs, riser-ready bases.

We work with touring artists, brand-led music activations, venues commissioning permanent or semi-permanent installations, and event producers running multi-show tours.

Touring artists & DJs

Multi-city sets, modular builds, photo-grade finishes, road-safe construction.

Brands sponsoring music

Branded DJ facades, stage skirts, sponsor activations integrated into the build.

Venues

Permanent installs, semi-permanent club builds, riser systems and B-stages.

Producers & promoters

One-off festival stages, branded set pieces, full production builds.

South Shore Woodworking is a custom fabrication shop in Center Moriches, on Long Island's south fork, ninety minutes from midtown Manhattan. Alex Vieco started the shop building one-of-a-kind furniture and salvaged-wood commissions for Long Island homeowners. The work taught him how to make almost anything out of wood, metal, and finish — and over the last several years, it has quietly turned into something different.

Today South Shore builds brand activation fabrications, experiential marketing sets, pop-up retail environments, custom retail displays, stage and DJ booth installations, mobile bars, catering pieces, and event sets for brand-activation agencies, experiential marketing firms, in-house corporate marketing teams, and the artists and DJs who tour them. We've shipped builds that travel between Brooklyn and Boston, branded environments that load in on a Friday and have to look perfect by 6 PM, and DJ booths that have to survive four shows on the road and still photograph cleanly for Instagram.

The reason agencies call a shop on Long Island instead of one in Brooklyn is the same reason brands call us instead of a national exhibit house: when you call, you reach the person running the saw.

There are no account managers between Alex and the build. There is no subcontracted second shop you've never met. The person designing the install is the person installing it. Most of our projects fall between $20,000 and $40,000, on timelines that respect the fact that load-in is a real date and you cannot reschedule it.

South Shore is fully equipped for CNC, hand-built millwork, finishing, and on-site install across NYC, Long Island, Brooklyn, northern New Jersey, and lower Connecticut. Larger jobs travel further on a case-by-case basis. We work directly with agency producers and brand-side marketing teams, and we work directly with the artists and creative directors who hire us without an agency in between. Marquee letter rentals — the original side of the business — are still available; the rest of the site is what we build now.
